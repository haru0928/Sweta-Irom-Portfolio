Approach

From line drawing to lived-in space

Two disciplines, one job: make a design legible — to the contractor reading a section, and to the client who needs to see the room before it exists.
2D drafting

Construction-ready, not just pretty

Plans, sections, elevations and details drawn to a standard a contractor can build from directly — dimensioned, layered, and coordinated with structural and MEP consultants.

3D visualization

Light, material, and doubt removed

Photoreal renders and walkthroughs built early enough to change the design, not just present it — tested against real sun angles and material samples from the valley.

Handoff

Built for the next person

Every drawing set and model is organized and layered for the architect or contractor who picks it up next — clean files, not just a clean render.
